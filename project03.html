no cage.

No Cage is a non-profit organziation dedicated to the advocacy for zoo animals. Bringing awareness to the risks animals face, finding zoo alternatives, and studying the ethicalness of individual zoos are all apart of No Cage's mission.

Created in Web Design I, No Cage was a semester-long project done with my partner, Ava Zaffarano. We both felt that there needed to be a way for parents, educators, students, and families to be able to easily access and find alternatives to zoos.

As users go through the website, they are able to read about the various conditions that zoo animals live in and the lasting impact they have. Users are also able to look up their locals zoos on the "Zoo Grading System" and get feedback as to how ethical they are. They can then find alternatives to the zoo in the "Beyond the Zoo" section.
