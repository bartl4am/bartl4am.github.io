ABOUT

I have grown up fascinated with the arts. I loved dancing, drawing, coloring, singing, playing dress-up, anything that allowed me to be someone else. I would force my cousins, who were 10 years older than me, to always be part of dance routines I created. I know listening to a 6 year old boss you around was the highlight of their day. Since then, my passion for the arts has grown and changed. Drawing stick figures turned into picture-perfect portrait drawings. Writing my name in the fanciest ways I could imagine evolved into a love for typography.

The closest people throughout my life have always been my family, friends, and art teachers. They saw the potential in me that I had yet to discover until now. I have them to thank for motivating me to be the designer I am today. Art has always given me the freedom to express myself. I want my art to reflect my love for the craft. I want my art to reflect my love for expression. I want my art to reflect my passion.

I believe art is about making a statement. I want to invoke emotion. I want to make people angry. I want them to feel something when they lay eyes on my work. I hope to inspire just as much as I hope to anger.
