“Bright Route” is a small-town coffee shop created for the New Venture Challenge. Their business mission is to create communities were Starbucks does not. They focus on smaller, rural communities. Along with a brick and mortar, they have a traveling "coffee kiosk" that would set up at various high schools in order to draw in business.
